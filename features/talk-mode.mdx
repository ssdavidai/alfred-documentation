## A butler who calls

Alfred Talk gives your Alfred instance a voice. Alfred can call you for briefings, reminders, and check-ins — and you can call Alfred's dedicated phone number any time you need something.

This isn't a robotic assistant reading from a script. Alfred speaks with the calm, warm, dry-witted composure of a proper butler — unflappable, attentive, and always prepared.

## How it works

Alfred Talk is powered by ElevenLabs voice synthesis and a dedicated phone number provisioned for your instance.

### Alfred calls you

Alfred initiates voice calls when the moment calls for it:

- **Morning briefings** — Your day ahead, summarised and delivered by voice
- **Reminders** — Tasks due, meetings approaching, commitments to keep
- **Onboarding touchpoints** — During your first days, Alfred checks in to make sure everything is working
- **The Day 30 envelope** — A milestone moment in your Alfred experience, delivered personally

### You call Alfred

Pick up the phone and dial Alfred's number any time. Ask questions, share updates, give instructions — Alfred listens, understands, and acts. Anything you share by voice flows through the same pipeline as text: the Curator processes it, records are created, and your vault grows.

## The Pennyworth persona

Alfred speaks as Alfred Pennyworth — the archetypal butler. Calm and warm. Precise without being cold. Occasionally dry-witted, never sarcastic. Unflappable in all circumstances.

This isn't a gimmick. The persona creates a consistent, trustworthy presence that makes voice interactions feel natural rather than transactional.

## Setting up Alfred Talk

Alfred Talk is enabled as an integration from your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Navigate to [alfred.black/dashboard](https://alfred.black/dashboard) and find the Integrations section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Alfred Talk">
    Toggle Alfred Talk on. Alfred provisions your dedicated phone number automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="ElevenLabs configuration">
    Alfred provides guided setup for ElevenLabs voice synthesis. Follow the prompts to connect your ElevenLabs account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start talking">
    Your dedicated phone number is ready. Alfred can now call you, and you can call Alfred.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What's included

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Dedicated phone number** | Included with Alfred Talk — no extra provisioning needed |
| **ElevenLabs voice** | Alfred provides setup guidance; requires an ElevenLabs account |
| **Inbound calls** | Call Alfred any time on your dedicated number |
| **Outbound calls** | Alfred calls you for briefings, reminders, and key moments |

## When Alfred calls

Alfred doesn't call arbitrarily. Voice calls are reserved for moments where a call is genuinely the right medium:

- Time-sensitive reminders where a notification might be missed
- Morning briefings that set the tone for your day
- Milestone moments in your onboarding journey
- Situations where a conversation is more efficient than a message

Everything Alfred says on a call is processed through the standard pipeline — transcribed, structured, and added to your vault.
